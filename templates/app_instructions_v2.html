Application usage instructions

To get access to the application

  1. Go to The app homepage
  2. Click on "Sign in with google".
  3. Sign in with a google account of your choice.
  4. If your account is not preapproved, contact us via email, telling us what google account you used so that we can approve your account in the system. Until this happens, you will not have access to the application.

After your account has been approved, and any time you want to upload data

All your data are uploaded via the same form. In order you will need to provide the following information:
  1. The project ID allocated to you (if you are assigned a single project ID this will be automatically selected for you )
  2. Whether you will run your own sequencing or you will use SCRIPPS (via SPUN)
  3. Your project information (Like "Country", "Expedition lead" etc )
  4. Information about each of your samples (what we call "Metadata") (Like coordinates, landuse, sampleID etc)
  5. (If you did your own sequencing:) SequencerIDs. This will help us match your sequencing data to your samples and identify amplicon regions. This step is not required if you used SCRIPPS
  6. (If you did your own sequencing:) The sequencing files themselves. These need to be files of type .fastq.gz . This step is not required if you used SCRIPPS

VERY IMPORTANT: You don't need to restart the process each time you login. You can always continue from the point you left the previous time

How to upload your data

  1. Go to the app homepage.
  2. (If you are not signed in) Sign in with google.
  3. (If you haven't submitted anything yet: ) Click on "Data submission form"
    OR
    (If you have started the process: ) Click on "Previous uploads of the user " and then select the process you have already started
  4. Follow the instructions in the form so that you can provide your data

Download your data

Once your data have been processed, a google bucket will be created where they will be stored.
You will be given access to all that data, including the fastqc/multiqc reports that have been generated
Please note: You will be contacted when your data are ready.
  1. Go to the app homepage.
  2. (If you are not signed in) Sign in with google.
  3. Click on the button "My data"
  4. You will see a list of the buckets to which you have been given access
  5. On each bucket you have access, you will see a link saying "Generate file". Click it
  6. At first it will tell you "File being generated. Please wait". So, please wait.
  7. Once the process is finished, the message will change to a link saying "Download"
  8. Click on the "Download" to initiate your download
  9. The file will expire after 24 hours. If you visit the page after that, you will have to generate a new file.
